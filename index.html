About Me

I'm Catherine Gosmeyer. I'm a NASA-contracted scientific software developer and analyst, demi-planetary scientist, and geospatial information science graduate student. Python is my language of choice. I'm also an editor for the Astrophysics Source Code Library and webmaster for the American Astronomical Society Division for Planetary Sciences (DPS). I'm passionate about space policy and advocate with the Planetary Society and DPS for steady, reliable funding for the exploration of the Solar System.
